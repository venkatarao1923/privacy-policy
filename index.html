Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Summary: ClickFlow Pro is a gesture automation tool for accessibility purposes. We do not sell your data and collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

1. Who We Are

ClickFlow Pro ("we", "our", "the app") is an accessibility-focused gesture automation app designed to assist users with repetitive interactions, motor impairments, or productivity automation. The app is operated independently and can be contacted at the email provided in the Google Play Store listing.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data required to provide our services:

  • Anonymous Device ID: A unique hardware identifier (Android ID) to manage your account and coin balance securely.
  • Firebase Auth UID: Anonymous authentication token — no email or password is required.
  • Coin Balance & Purchase Records: Stored in Firebase Firestore to track your in-app currency and subscriptions.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Google AdMob collects limited data to serve rewarded ads. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT record what apps you use or what you click.
  • We do NOT store screen recordings, screenshots, or any visual data.
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with third-party advertisers beyond what Google AdMob requires for serving ads.

4. Accessibility Service Usage

ClickFlow Pro uses Android's Accessibility Service to perform touch gestures on your behalf. This is solely to automate screen interactions as instructed by you. We do not log, store, or transmit any content observed through the accessibility service.

5. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. Transaction records are handled entirely by Google.

6. Data Retention

Your coin balance and subscription data is stored in Firebase Firestore and linked to your anonymous device ID. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us through the Play Store. Upon deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Google Play Store page or at the developer email listed there.
